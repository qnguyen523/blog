Welcome to My Portfolio

Quoc Nguyen is a Vietnamese software engineer and businessman. He was born and raised in Vietnam. He was educated in the U.S for the major of Computer Science. He realizes Vietnam is a developing country with a lack of high technology. Workstyles in many firms and government agencies have been outdated. Their employees are using too much paper to keep record. Their work is chaotic and tangled. I believe their work can be more organized and manageable with the implementation of technology. Vietnam is converting into digital system. With an increasing need of IT, he takes a leap of faith to quit his 9-5 job in America and return to his homeland. He is passionate to contribute his talent to build computer system. Because of poor quality of life and work in Vietnam, he is inspired to utilize software in a daily basis to improve productivity and human life quality. Aside from his work, his hobbies include playing piano, reading, travelling, and helping others overcome their life difficulty. Observing many Vietnamese in rural area to survive in extreme poor life quality, he feels great compassion and strong desire to revamp their way of life.
